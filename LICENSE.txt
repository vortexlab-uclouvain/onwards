OnWaRDS - Online Wake Rapid Dynamic Simulator - An online dynamic wake modeling framework bringing together Lagrangian wake modeling and flow sensing that allows to reconstruct estimated snapshots of the flow field across the wind farm based on the information gathered by the wind turbines.
